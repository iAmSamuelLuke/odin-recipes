Smash Burgers

This succulent, delicious, mouth watering burger will have you screaming for more. Packed with delicious ingredients and a whole lotta love.

Ingredients

  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 LB ground chuck beef
  • parchment paper
  • salt
  • 4 slices American cheese
  • burger toppings

Steps

  1. preheat grill
  2. spread butter on buns
  3. form meat into 8 loosely packed balls
